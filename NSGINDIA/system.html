RECRUITMENT PROCESS


Step 1 :- Submission Of Application Form

  • - You can either collect the application form from our head office or you can Click Here to download it.
  • - Fill it up with all the correct details and submit it to the nearest head office with each one of your required document's self-attested copy asap.

    Step 2 :- Interview Call

  • - Please wait for an interview call from our side.
  • - Your interview details will be send to you through the email-id you filled up in your application form.
  • - You need to visit our office 30 mins ago before your interview timing with all of your required original documents for verification.

    Step 3 :- Written Test

  • - After selection in interview process, you have to give a written test on specific date provided by us.
  • - Questions in test will be of basic level about Scouting and Team Management.

    Step 4 :- Congratulations!

  • - After successfully clearing your aptitude test you will receive an appointment letter from our side by mail.
  • - You need to report to our head office on date and at time as mentioned in your appointment letter.

    • For any other kind of queries feel free to Contact Us
