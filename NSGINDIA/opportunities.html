SCOUTS MASTER
  • JOB PROFILE :-

    • Scout Masters are responsible for Boys training and making arrangements for Scouting campaign Program(SCP).
    They may also be appointed in various schools for Students Training Program(STP).

  • QUALIFICATIONS :-

    • Graduation or equivalent Completed(or in final year)

  • PAY SCALE :-
